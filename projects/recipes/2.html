How to Make Kompot
Steps What to Do
1 Get fruits like apples, strawberries, cherries, or even plums (about 2 cups total).
2 Cut big fruits like apples or plums into small pieces. Remove pits or seeds.
3 Fill a pot with about 6 cups of water and add all the fruit.
4 Add 4–5 tablespoons of sugar, depending on how sweet you want it to be.
5 Put it on boil, then lower the heat and let it sit for 15–20 minutes.
6 Turn off the stove and let it cool down for a bit.
7 Strain out the fruit or leave it in and then pour into a pitcher.
8 Chill in the fridge or serve warm.

Tips

  • Choose a bunch of fruits.
  • Don't over-boil.
  • Flavor with spices and citrus.
  • Save the leftover fruit.
