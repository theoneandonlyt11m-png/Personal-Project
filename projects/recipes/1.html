How to Make A Borscht
Steps What to Do
1 Peel and cut 2 beets, 1 potato, 1 carrot, and 1 onion into small pieces.
2 Put a little oil in a big pot. Cook the onion and carrot for 3–5 minutes.
3 Add the beets and potatoes. Pour in water until everything is covered (about 5 cups).
4 Add 1 spoon of tomato paste.
5 Boil for 20–30 minutes, until the veggies are soft.
6 Add a couple of chopped cabbage. Cook for about 10 to 13 min.
7 Add salt and pepper.
8 Put in a spoon of sour cream when you serve it.

Tips

  • Use vinegar or lemon juice for color.
  • Create a good broth.
  • Don't add sour cream to the whole pot.
  • Serve with sour cream and fresh herbs.
